# Trang Thai Minh Chung Thuc Te

Tai lieu nay ghi ro nhung minh chung nao da co san trong repo va nhung minh chung nao can duoc tao tren may co MySQL.

## 1. Minh chung da hoan thanh trong repo

- Bo noi dung bao cao tu Chuong 1 den Chuong 8
- Data Dictionary chi tiet
- Danh sach index
- Truy van mau
- Huong dan backup/restore
- Huong dan EXPLAIN
- So do ERD dang Mermaid
- Ghi chu sai lech schema-code

## 2. Minh chung chua the tao trong workspace hien tai

Môi truong hien tai khong co:

- lenh `mysql`
- lenh `mysqldump`

Do do, cac minh chung sau chua the tao truc tiep trong workspace nay:

- anh ket qua chay `EXPLAIN`
- anh ket qua backup bang `mysqldump`
- anh ket qua restore
- anh import schema vao DBMS
- anh du lieu seed sau khi nap vao database

## 3. Cach bo sung de bao cao dat muc hoan chinh cao

Can thuc hien tren mot may da cai MySQL:

1. Tao database `lms`
2. Chay schema `backend/lms.sql`
3. Chay seed SQL
4. Chay cac truy van trong `docs/sql/explain_queries.sql`
5. Chup man hinh ket qua `EXPLAIN`
6. Chay backup/restore va chup minh chung

## 4. Cach viet trung thuc trong bao cao

Neu chua bo sung minh chung thuc te, nen viet:

- "Bao cao de xuat quy trinh va lenh thuc hien backup/restore"
- "Cac truy van EXPLAIN da duoc chuan bi san de thu nghiem"

Khong nen viet:

- "He thong da duoc backup thanh cong" neu chua co anh/output thuc te
- "Da trien khai EXPLAIN va do duoc hieu nang" neu chua co ket qua chay that
